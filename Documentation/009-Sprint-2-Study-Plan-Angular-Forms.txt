Angular Forms study plan - w/ paid resources
Week 1: Introduction to Angular Forms

Read the official Angular documentation on forms: 
https://angular.io/guide/forms-overview
Watch the "Angular Forms Tutorial" series by Fireship on YouTube: 
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UxMfzL-9kQA&list=PL0vfts4VzfNiQWwxfkltqY1HuC1BcnyAQ
Complete the "Getting Started with Angular Forms" tutorial on Scrimba: 
https://scrimba.com/learn/angularforms
Week 2: Template-driven Forms

Read the official Angular documentation on Template-driven forms: 
https://angular.io/guide/forms
Watch the "Angular Template-driven Forms" tutorial by Traversy Media on YouTube: 
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oa9cnWTpqP8
Complete the "Angular Template-driven Forms" tutorial on Udemy: 
https://www.udemy.com/course/angular-template-driven-forms/
Week 3: Reactive Forms

Read the official Angular documentation on Reactive forms: 
https://angular.io/guide/reactive-forms
Watch the "Angular Reactive Forms" tutorial by Fireship on YouTube: 
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cdlbFEsCk7o
Complete the "Angular Reactive Forms" tutorial on Pluralsight: 
https://www.pluralsight.com/courses/angular-2-reactive-forms
Week 4: Advanced Forms Topics

Read about form validation, form submission, and handling form events in Angular
Watch the "Angular Forms Deep Dive" series by Fireship on YouTube: 
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3YDiloj8_d0&list=PL0vfts4VzfNj_IKGNsVjTqTqU6VvTBlxB
